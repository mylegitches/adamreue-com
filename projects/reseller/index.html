Reseller

AI-Powered Marketplace Listing Automation

Personal Project 2024

Overview

Reseller is a Python-based automation tool that uses AI vision to process folders of product images and automatically generate optimized sales listings for multiple online marketplaces. The system analyzes product images to generate descriptions, determines fair pricing based on market data, and creates compelling sales ads.

Key Features

🖼️

AI Image Analysis

Computer vision algorithms analyze product images to identify items and generate detailed descriptions

💰

Dynamic Pricing

Market-aware pricing algorithm that suggests optimal listing prices based on competitor analysis

📝

Automated Listings

Generates optimized product listings with titles, descriptions, and keywords for multiple platforms

🏪

Multi-Platform

Support for major online marketplaces including eBay, Facebook Marketplace, and others
