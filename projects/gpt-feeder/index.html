GPT Feeder

AI Workflow Automation Tool

Personal Project 2024

Overview

GPT Feeder is a Python automation tool designed to eliminate the tedious process of manually copying screenshots to AI chat interfaces. With a simple hotkey, it captures your screen, automatically opens your preferred AI interface, and pastes the image directly into the chat, allowing you to focus on your actual work rather than repetitive tasks.

Key Features

📸

Instant Capture

One-click screenshot capture with area selection and instant AI interface integration

🎯

Multi-Platform Support

Works with ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and other major AI chat platforms

⚡

Workflow Optimization

Eliminates copy-paste workflows, saving time and reducing context switching

🔧

Customizable

Configurable hotkeys, AI platform preferences, and capture settings

Technology Stack

Core Language

System Integration
